VOW RENEWAL PHOTOGRAPHY, ANSWERED

What couples ask us first.

How much does a Maui vow renewal photographer cost?

Vow renewal sessions start at $249 for smaller, private ceremonies and run higher for larger family celebrations. Current pricing is on our Sessions & Pricing page.

Can our children or family be part of the vow renewal photos?

Yes. Many vow renewals include children and extended family as part of the celebration.

What is the best time of day for a Maui vow renewal?

Golden hour into sunset is most popular for its light and atmosphere, though sunrise ceremonies offer a quieter, softer alternative.

How far in advance should we book?

Several weeks to a few months ahead is recommended, especially for sunset time slots during peak travel season.

Do you photograph vow renewals outside of Wailea?

Yes. While based in Wailea, we photograph vow renewals throughout Makena and South Maui depending on your vision.
