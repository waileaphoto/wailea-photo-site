MATERNITY PHOTOGRAPHY, ANSWERED

What expecting parents ask us first.

How much does a Maui maternity photography session cost?

Maternity sessions start at $249. Current pricing and availability are listed on our Sessions & Pricing page.

What is the best trimester for a Maui maternity photoshoot?

Most clients book between 28 and 36 weeks, when the belly is well shaped but travel and standing for a session are still comfortable.

Sunrise or sunset for maternity photos?

Both work beautifully. Sunrise gives fresh, soft color with fewer crowds; sunset gives warmer, more dramatic light.

Can partners and other children be included?

Yes. Many maternity sessions include partners and older siblings as part of the family's growing story.

Do you offer an outfit change during the session?

Many sessions can include time for an outfit change — just mention it when you book so we can plan accordingly.
