WEDDING PHOTOGRAPHY, ANSWERED

What couples ask us first.

How much does a Maui wedding photographer cost?

Pricing depends on coverage length and whether it’s an elopement or a full wedding day. Sessions start around $249 for shorter coverage and run higher for full wedding-day packages — current pricing is on our Sessions & Pricing page.

Do you photograph elopements as well as full weddings?

Yes. We photograph everything from small, intimate elopements for two to full wedding-day coverage with family and guests.

What is the best time of day for Maui wedding photos?

Late afternoon into golden hour and sunset gives the softest light and the true island color Maui is known for. We help you plan ceremony timing around it.

How far in advance should we book?

Several months ahead is recommended, especially for peak wedding season and sunset time slots, which book first.

Do you photograph weddings outside of Wailea?

Yes. While based in Wailea, we regularly photograph weddings and elopements throughout Makena, Kapalua and South Maui.
