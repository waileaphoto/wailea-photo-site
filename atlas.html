The Wailea Photo Atlas

The Art, Science, and Local Knowledge Behind Portrait Photography in Maui

A GROWING MAUI REFERENCE

Twenty-five years of field knowledge, shared.

For more than 25 years, our family has photographed families, couples, children, and weddings across Wailea, Makena, and South Maui.

During thousands of portrait sessions, we have learned that creating a meaningful photograph requires much more than choosing a beautiful beach. It requires an understanding of light, weather, wind, tides, color, optics, family dynamics, and the quiet moments that transform a photograph into a memory.

The Wailea Photo Atlas is where we share that knowledge.

This is not a traditional photography blog. It is a growing reference devoted to the art and engineering of portrait photography in Maui—from choosing the right beach and time of day to understanding professional lighting, Leica optics, Kodak film, natural skin tones, and the craft behind timeless family portraits.

OUR GUIDING IDEA

Photography at the intersection of Art and Engineering.

Art gives a portrait meaning.

Engineering gives the photographer the control to preserve that meaning under changing and sometimes difficult conditions.

The Atlas brings those two disciplines together. Every article is written from direct experience and designed to help families make informed decisions while also contributing useful knowledge to photographers and anyone interested in the craft of portraiture.

The Wailea Photo Atlas will continue to grow as we document what thousands of Maui portrait sessions have taught us.
