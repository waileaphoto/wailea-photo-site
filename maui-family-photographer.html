CHOOSING YOUR LIGHT

Sunrise or sunset — which is right for your family?

Sunrise with Max is quiet, quick and golden — a 20-minute session from $249 for families who want soft morning light without giving up a whole evening. First Half and Last Half of Sunset each run 30 minutes, timed around the evening's changing color, from $399 and $499. The Turquoise + Water Experience gives your family 45 minutes at one of Maui's clearest lagoons, from $499, for those who want more time in the water. Every option includes 50 or more professionally edited images, and large multi-generational groups can book The Large Family Legacy, a full hour with 100+ images.

FAMILY PHOTOGRAPHY, ANSWERED

What families ask us first.

What is a Maui family photographer session like?

Relaxed and unhurried. Sessions typically take place during golden hour near sunrise or sunset, when the light is softest, with gentle guidance so every generation feels comfortable in front of the camera.

How much does a family photography session cost in Maui?

Sunrise and sunset sessions start at $249, and include 50 or more professionally edited images. Current pricing and availability are listed on our Sessions & Pricing page.

Can you photograph multi-generational family reunions?

Yes. Multi-generation sessions are common — just let us know your group size in advance so we can plan enough time for both group and individual combinations. Our Large Family Legacy session is built specifically for gatherings of 10 or more.

What should our family wear for Maui photos?

Neutral, earth-toned colors photograph best: soft blues, sand, beige, sage and warm pastels. Coordinated, not identical, outfits give the most timeless look.

Do you photograph families staying outside Wailea?

Yes. While based in Wailea, we work throughout Makena and South Maui depending on light and location.

What happens if it rains or the weather doesn't cooperate?

We reschedule at no charge. Hawaii weather moves fast, and a passing shower rarely lasts long, but if conditions genuinely won't work we'll find you another time during your trip.

How do I book, and how fast will I get my photos?

Choose a session and time directly on our Sessions & Pricing page, or call or text 808.298.5188 and we'll help you find a slot. Most galleries are delivered within 48 hours of your session.
