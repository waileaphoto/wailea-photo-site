BEFORE YOU BOOK

A few things worth knowing.

One thing first: please don't email to ask about availability — our booking calendar updates in real time, so the fastest way to see open dates is the live booking link.

What is a family beach photoshoot in Maui like?

Relaxed and unhurried. Sessions typically take place during golden hour near sunset, when the light is softest. You'll get gentle guidance so kids feel comfortable and parents can relax — the goal is natural, candid images rather than stiff, posed ones.

How much does a family beach photoshoot cost?

It depends on session length, number of people and the type of session. Sunrise and sunset sessions start at $249; portrait series and weddings run higher. Current pricing and availability are on our Sessions & Pricing page.

When is the best time of day for photos?

Golden hour — the last 60 to 90 minutes before sunset — creates soft skin tones, reduced shadows and a cinematic glow that's especially flattering for families with young children. A few of our sessions are also offered at sunrise for the same quality of light with fewer crowds.

What should we wear?

Neutral and earth-toned colors photograph best: soft blues, sand, beige, sage, muted greens and warm pastels. Avoid bright neon colors, large logos, or fully matching outfits — coordinated (not identical) clothing gives the most timeless look.

How many people can be included?

Immediate and extended family are both welcome. Multi-generation sessions are popular — just let us know your group size in advance so we can plan enough time and space for both group and individual combinations.

Do you help choose the beach location?

Yes. We select the best location based on light, wind, crowd levels and accessibility, working across beaches throughout Wailea, Makena and South Maui to match your family's needs.

What if our kids don't cooperate?

Completely normal, and expected. We build sessions around real family dynamics, not perfect behavior — some of the most meaningful images happen between the "posed" moments.

How far in advance should we book?

Several weeks to months ahead, especially during peak travel season. Booking early ensures availability during golden hour and gives us time to plan location, wardrobe and session details together.

Are these sessions good for vacations, proposals and reunions?

Yes — vacations, reunions, anniversaries, proposals and milestone trips are some of our most common sessions. Many families choose to document their time in Maui as a lasting keepsake of being together.

Do you photograph families staying outside Wailea?

Yes. Our Turquoise + Water session, for example, is located near the airport. While based in Wailea, we work throughout South Maui and nearby areas depending on light and conditions.
